Nutrition Diets for Different Age Groups

Children (5-12 years old)

  • Breakfast: Cereal with milk and dried fruits (250 calories)
  • Lunch: Macaroni with tomato sauce and steamed vegetables (350 calories)
  • Dinner: Grilled fish with basmati rice and fresh vegetables (400 calories)

Teenagers (13-18 years old)

  • Breakfast: Omelette with spinach and onions, with whole grain toast (300 calories)
  • Lunch: Greek salad with tomatoes, olives, feta cheese, and grilled chicken (450 calories)
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and roasted asparagus (400 calories)

Adults (25-50 years old)

  • Breakfast: Yogurt with granola and fresh fruits (350 calories)
  • Lunch: Chicken stir-fry with brown rice and mixed vegetables (500 calories)
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with sweet potato fries and green salad (600 calories)

Elderly (60+ years old)

  • Breakfast: Whole wheat toast with butter and honey, and a cup of tea (200 calories)
  • Lunch: Vegetable soup with beans and whole grain bread (300 calories)
  • Dinner: Grilled fish with mashed potatoes and steamed broccoli (400 calories)
